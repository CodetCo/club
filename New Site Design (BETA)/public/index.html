Codet Club

Held every Wednesday at lunch, in room 617.

Interested in computer science?

Are you intrigued by computer science but haven't had the chance to explore it? Join the Codet Club and learn the basics of various computer science topics. Through learning about a multitude of languages and how they're used, you will not only be introduced to computer science, but you will also be provided with the skills to create programs from scratch.

Some examples of the skills you will learn include: creating and deploying a professional-looking website, programming in Java/Python/C++, using Github to save and collaborate on code, and more!

Our Mission

The mission of Mountain View High School's Codet Club is to both teach members the basics of various computer science topics, such as in web development and object oriented programming, and give them the opportunity to teach these skills at Blach Intermediate School. For more information, see our club constitution here.

What we provide

Organized Lessons

There will be lessons every week with a dedicated teacher who has experience with teaching and the topic. These lessons will be accompanied with slides created to teach at this club. Material will cover the basics of various programming topics (Web Development, Python, C++, Java, Github, etc.)

Teaching Opportunities

Teaching is a skill that is extremely useful no matter what career you choose to pursue. You will be given the opportunity to teach/mentor at Blach every Monday after school. Furthermore, the Codet Summer Camp will be organized by the club members, giving them the chance to organize and teach at an event.
